Marvel is a comic book franchise and currently world’s leading movie franchise. It is owned by disney and also has it’s own movie studio i.e. Marvel Studios.


Marvel was first established in 1939 as timely publications and has seen all ups and downs in its long career. It currently is well known all over the world for it’s comics and movies. Marvel studio’s president Kevin Feige has produced all marvel movies since 2008’s Iron Man.


Marvel have been successful in creating its cinematic universe (Marvel Cinematic Universe aka MCU) and is the most successful movie franchise at box office.
